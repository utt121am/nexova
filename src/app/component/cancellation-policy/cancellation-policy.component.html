Cancellation Policy

Understand our cancellation rules and refund process for your NEXOVA bus bookings.

General Cancellation Policy

At NEXOVA, we understand that plans can change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to both our customers and our bus operators.

Important: Cancellation charges vary depending on when you cancel your ticket relative to the departure time. Partial cancellations (for some passengers in a booking) are allowed in most cases.

Cancellation Charges

The cancellation charges are calculated as a percentage of the ticket fare or a flat fee, whichever is higher:

Time Before Departure Cancellation Charges Refund Amount
More than 48 hours 10% of fare or ₹50 (whichever is higher) 90% of fare or (fare - ₹50)
24 to 48 hours 25% of fare or ₹100 (whichever is higher) 75% of fare or (fare - ₹100)
6 to 24 hours 50% of fare 50% of fare
Less than 6 hours 100% of fare (No refund) No refund

Note: For premium and luxury buses, additional cancellation charges may apply as per operator policy.

How to Cancel Your Booking

Online Cancellation

You can cancel your booking through our website or mobile app:

  • Go to 'My Bookings' section
  • Select the booking you wish to cancel
  • Click on 'Cancel Ticket'
  • Confirm the cancellation
  • Your refund will be processed automatically

Customer Support

Refund Process

Refund Timeline

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 7-10 business days
  • Net Banking: 3-5 business days
  • UPI Payments: 1-3 business days
  • NEXOVA Wallet: Instant credit

Refund Method

Refunds are processed through the original payment method used for booking. If the original payment method is not available, we may issue the refund via NEXOVA Wallet or bank transfer.

Important: In case of bus cancellations by the operator, you will receive a 100% refund automatically within 24 hours.

Special Cases

Partial Cancellations

If you need to cancel tickets for only some passengers in a booking:

  • Partial cancellations are allowed up to 6 hours before departure
  • Cancellation charges apply only to the cancelled seats
  • The remaining passengers can travel as scheduled

No-Show Policy

If you don't show up for your bus:

  • No refund will be issued
  • The full ticket amount will be forfeited
  • You may be able to board a later bus if seats are available (subject to charges)
