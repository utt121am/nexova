Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about booking, payments, cancellations, and more with NEXOVA Bus Booking.

Booking & Tickets

How do I book a bus ticket on NEXOVA?

Booking a bus ticket on NEXOVA is simple. Just enter your departure and destination cities, select your travel date, and choose from the available buses. After selecting your preferred bus and seats, proceed to payment to complete your booking.

Can I book tickets for multiple passengers?

Yes, you can book tickets for up to 6 passengers in a single transaction. Simply select the number of passengers when choosing your seats.

Is there a mobile app for NEXOVA?

Yes, NEXOVA has a mobile app available for both Android and iOS devices. You can download it from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.

Payments & Refunds

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI payments (Google Pay, PhonePe, etc.), and NEXOVA Wallet.

How long does it take to process a refund?

Refunds are typically processed within 7-10 business days. The exact time depends on your bank or payment provider.

Are there any cancellation charges?

Cancellation charges vary depending on when you cancel your ticket. Generally, cancellations made more than 24 hours before departure have lower charges. Please check our cancellation policy for detailed information.

Travel & Boarding

What documents do I need to board the bus?

You need to present either a printed copy of your ticket or show the e-ticket on your mobile device along with a valid government-issued photo ID.

How early should I arrive at the boarding point?

We recommend arriving at least 30 minutes before the scheduled departure time to ensure a smooth boarding process.

Can I track my bus in real-time?

Yes, you can track your bus in real-time through our website or mobile app. Just go to the "Track Bus" section and enter your booking details.

Still have questions?

If you didn't find the answer you were looking for, our customer support team is available 24/7 to assist you.
